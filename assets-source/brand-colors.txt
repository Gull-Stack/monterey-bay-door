# MONTEREY BAY DOOR - Brand Colors

## Primary Color Palette
- **White:** #FFFFFF (primary background/text)
- **Dark Teal:** #051E21 (primary brand color - dark sophisticated)
- **Sage Green:** #94AB69 (accent color - natural/professional)

## Color Usage Notes
- Dark Teal (#051E21): Headers, primary buttons, logo elements
- Sage Green (#94AB69): Accent elements, secondary buttons, highlights
- White (#FFFFFF): Background, negative space, contrast text

## Color Harmony
This palette creates a professional, trustworthy feel perfect for commercial access control:
- Dark teal conveys security and reliability
- Sage green adds approachability and growth
- White provides clean, professional contrast

## Technical Specs
- All colors web-safe and accessible
- High contrast ratios for readability
- Professional B2B aesthetic aligned with dwellpaint.com framework
